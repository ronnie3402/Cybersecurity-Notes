## 4. CRYPTOGRAPHY

---

## 4.1 Steganography

---

## steghide

Hides data inside:

- images  
- audio files  

---

## steghide (extract usage concept)

Used to embed or extract hidden payloads from media files.

---

## stegseek

Fast brute-force tool for steghide passwords.

---

## 4.2 Encryption & Privacy

---

## openssl

Used for:

- encryption / decryption  
- certificate generation  
- hashing  

---

## PGP / GPG

Used for:

- encrypted emails  
- file signing  
- secure communication  

---

## VeraCrypt

Disk encryption tool for:

- USB encryption  
- full disk encryption  
- secure containers  

---

## 4.3 Encoding Tools

---

## base64

Encoding method (NOT encryption).

Used for:

- safe data transfer  
- data representation in text format  

---

## CyberChef

Web-based forensic tool:

- encoding / decoding  
- encryption analysis  
- data transformation  

---

## 4.4 Hashing & Cracking

---

## hash-identifier

Identifies hash types.

---

## hashcat

GPU-based password cracking tool.

---

## John the Ripper

Dictionary + brute-force cracking tool.

---

## Online Tools

- CrackStation → hash lookup database  
- CyberChef → hash analysis
